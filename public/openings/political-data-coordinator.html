Political Data Coordinator
San Diego, CA      |      Remote     |      07 / 10 / 2020
About the Role
We are looking for a passionate political data coordinator to contribute to and help maintain Voterly, America’s most comprehensive political database. This individual will collect and organize data, and research various political subjects including legislation, campaign finance, elections, policy, government systems, issues, trends and more.
Why work at Vidaloop?
  • Make an impact through cutting-edge civic technology
  • Grow with an early stage startup
  • Flexible schedule (25-40 hours per week)
What You'll Do
  • Collect data from sources such as government databases, political organizations, public opinion surveys, and election results
  • Perform data entry and other clerical work as required to maintain and enhance Voterly’s political database
  • Present research by writing unbiased reports and giving presentations
  • Work closely with the development team to implement new features
  • Assist the marketing team with social media and blog/copywriting
  • Follow current political events to serve as the team’s political expert
Skills & Qualifications
Technical Requirements
  • B.A. / B.S. in political studies or related field (or comparable work experience)
  • Well-versed in civics and politics
  • Advanced written and verbal communication skills
  • Excellent analytical and critical thinking skills
  • Exceptional attention to detail
  • Proficiency in Microsoft Office and Google Business Suite (Docs, Sheets, Slides)
  • Proficiency in English
Strong Pluses
  • Work experience in civic tech space or political campaigns
  • Social media and/or marketing background
  • Thrive in early-stage startup environment
