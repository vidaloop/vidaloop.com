Product Manager
La Mesa, CA      |      Full Time      |      01 / 12 / 2020
About the Role
This individual is responsible for leading product planning and marketing for a new blockchain based election system. This system will be built from the ground up so we’re looking for a well-rounded individual who can also help grow the team.
What You'll Do
  • Define product strategy and roadmap
  • Gain deep understanding of stakeholders' perspectives (regulators, legislators, administrators, voters, etc.)
  • Identify and fill product gaps and generate new ideas that drive growth
  • Create buy-in for the product vision both internally and with key external partners
  • Translate product strategy into detailed requirements and prototypes
  • Develop product pricing and positioning strategies
  • Work with executives to identify and run beta and pilot programs
  • Represent the company by meeting with customers and soliciting feedback
Skills & Qualifications
Technical Requirements
  • Minimum of 5 years experience as a Product Manager
  • Demonstrated success defining and launching excellent products
  • Solid technical background with understanding or hands-on experience in software development and web technologies
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Proven ability to influence cross-functional teams
  • Ability to travel as needed
Strong Pluses
  • Election industry experience
  • Understanding of blockchain fundamentals
  • Experience writing PRDs for highly regulated products
Other Pluses
  • Thrive in early-stage startup environment
  • Demonstrated leadership ability and personal accountability
  • Self-motivated and resourceful individual
  • Curious, life long learner who enjoys solving interesting problems
  • Bonus: Politically and socially engaged; excited about the elections industry
