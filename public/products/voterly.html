Voterly is the only app you need to navigate the political landscape quickly and easily. It’s the most comprehensive database of over 150,000 politicians— with profiles that summarize track records, campaign finance, career history, contact details and more. Voterly takes the guesswork out of voting by centralizing your state’s upcoming elections, deadlines and requirements into a single voter information hub— where you can also manage your voter registration status.
