Why Vidaloop?
  • Make an impact. We’re creating tools that reduce voting barriers for everyday Americans because we believe that having more informed voters will strengthen our democracy.
  • Work with a modern tech stack. Microservices, Serverless, Typescript, and NoSQL aren’t just buzzwords. At Vidaloop, we’ve leveraged modern technologies on top of AWS to build a robust decoupled architecture that can grow with us. Continuous integration and automated deployments ensure that we spend our time building products instead of checking for regressions or fiddling with AWS configurations. We provide a technologically advanced environment with new Macbooks, ergonomic keyboards, and electronic height-adjustable desks.
  • Culture of freedom and responsibility. We hire motivated and intelligent people who are excited about the problems we’re solving and do great work. This means you won’t be micromanaged and you can choose the hours you work.
  • Shape the company, grow with us. Get in on the ground floor of a rapidly-growing startup. We’re looking for talented, well-rounded people to help us shape our company culture and blaze a trail in the civic tech space.
